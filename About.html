Our Restaurant website is built around one main idea: you are the chef. With our Build Your Meal feature, you can choose your base, add ingredients, set spice level and share health details so we can cook exactly the way you like it.

1. Build Your Meal

Start on the Build page and choose your base like rice, pasta, noodles, roti or salad. Then add veggies, protein, sauces and your favourite flavour profile.

2. Health Friendly

Share health information such as diabetes, BP or low-salt diet on the builder form so we can adjust oil, salt, sugar and ingredients for a safer, healthier plate.

3. Book & Enjoy

Use the Book Slot page to reserve your table with date, time and number of guests. Reach the restaurant, and your customized meal will be prepared fresh for you.
